Mango

Soil Fertility:

  • Soil Types: Mangoes can grow in a variety of soils, but deep, well-drained soils are ideal. Sandy loam or loamy soils are particularly favorable.
  • pH: A pH range of 5.5 to 7.5 is generally preferred.
  • Drainage: Good drainage is essential, as mango trees are susceptible to waterlogging.

Temperature:

  • Climate: Mangoes thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
  • Temperature Range: Optimal temperatures range from 22°C to 27°C.
  • Dry weather before flowering is beneficial.
  • Frost can be damaging.

Mango Varieties in India:

  • Alphonso: Known for its exceptional flavor and aroma.
  • Dashehari: A popular variety with a sweet taste.
  • Langra: Recognized for its unique flavor.
  • Kesar: Prized for its sweet taste and vibrant color.
  • Banganapalli: Known for its large size and sweet taste.
  • Amrapali: A hybrid variety, known for high yielding.
