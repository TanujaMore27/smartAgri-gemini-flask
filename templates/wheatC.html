Wheat Crop Management

Nutrients

  • High nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), potassium (K) requirements.
  • Organic matter is beneficial.

Pesticides & Fertilizers

  • Herbicides to control weeds.
  • Fungicides to prevent rust and mildew.
  • Fertilizers: NPK for strong growth and yield.

Water Requirements

  • Moderate water requirements, especially during tillering and grain filling.
  • Well-drained soil is essential to prevent waterlogging.

Growth & Harvest

  • Moderate growth rate.
  • Harvesting time is when grains are mature and dry.
