Tomato Crop Management

Nutrients

  • High nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), potassium (K) requirements.
  • Well-drained soil rich in organic matter.

Pesticides & Fertilizers

  • Insecticides for fruit borers, aphids, and whiteflies.
  • Fungicides to prevent fungal diseases like blight.
  • Fertilizers: NPK with calcium and magnesium for healthy fruit development.

Water Requirements

  • Moderate water requirements, but regular irrigation is necessary during flowering and fruit development.
  • Avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.

Growth & Harvest

  • Fast-growing crop, typically ready for harvest within 60-90 days.
  • Harvest when tomatoes turn fully red and firm.
