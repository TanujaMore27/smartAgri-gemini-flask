Tea Crop Management

Nutrients

  • Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), and Organic Matter.
  • Acidic soil is essential for tea growth.

Pesticides & Fertilizers

  • Fungicides and Insecticides.
  • Acidifying fertilizers to maintain soil acidity.

Water Requirements

  • High rainfall and humidity are essential.
  • Well-drained slopes prevent water stagnation.

Growth & Harvest

  • Continuous growth with regular plucking.
  • Harvesting involves plucking new leaf buds throughout the season.
