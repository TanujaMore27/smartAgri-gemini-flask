Barley

Soil Fertility:

  • Soil Types: Barley thrives in sandy loam to loamy soils and can tolerate saline and sodic conditions.
  • Acidic Soils: Generally not preferred for barley cultivation.
  • Nutrient Requirements: Requires a balanced supply of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, adjusted based on soil testing.

Temperature:

  • Climate: Barley is a Rabi (winter) crop that prefers a cool, dry climate.
  • Temperature Range: Optimal temperatures for growth are between 12°C and 15°C.
  • Higher temperatures around 30°C are suitable for maturity.
  • Frost can be detrimental, especially during flowering.

Barley Varieties in India:

  • Adaptability: Different varieties are suited for irrigated or rainfed conditions.
  • Yield Potential: Selection depends on disease resistance and local adaptability.
  • Variety Sources: Indian Institute of Wheat and Barley Research (IIWBR) and State Agricultural Universities provide variety recommendations.
