Cotton Crop Management

Nutrients:

  • Essential Nutrients: Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), and Micronutrients.
  • Fertilization: Balanced fertilization based on soil testing is essential.

Pesticides & Fertilizers:

  • Pesticides: Insecticides for bollworms, aphids, and other pests.
  • Herbicides: Used for weed control.
  • Fertilizers: NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) with occasional sulfur application.

Water Requirements:

  • Watering Needs: Moderate to high, especially during boll development.
  • Soil Drainage: Well-drained soils are essential to prevent root rot.

Growth & Harvest:

  • Growth Period: 5-8 months.
  • Harvesting: Boll opening marks harvest time.
