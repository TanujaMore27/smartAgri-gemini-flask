Tomato

Soil Fertility:

  • Soil Types: Tomatoes thrive in well-drained, fertile soils, particularly sandy loam or loamy soils. Good drainage is critical to prevent waterlogging.
  • pH: An optimal pH range is between 6.0 and 7.0. Maintaining this pH level ensures proper nutrient uptake.
  • Nutrient Requirements: Tomatoes require a balanced supply of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, as well as micronutrients. Organic matter enrichment is highly beneficial.
