Cotton Seeds

Soil Fertility:

  • Ideal Soil: Cotton thrives in deep, fertile, well-drained soils with good water-holding capacity. Loamy or sandy loam soils are generally preferred. Cotton is sensitive to waterlogging.
  • pH: Optimal pH is slightly acidic to slightly alkaline (around 6.0 to 8.0). Soil testing is essential to determine nutrient levels and pH.
  • Nutrients: Cotton requires essential nutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and various micronutrients. Balanced fertilization based on soil test results is vital for healthy growth and yield.
